Come live your best life

Everything we do revolves around Magento, solving our clients’ problems, and preventing them from occurring in the first place.

Akoova has a proud history with eCommerce; its founders are credited with developing one of the first eCommerce SaaS platforms – Venda. They’ve also experienced first-hand the problems faced by high-growth retailers. This knowledge and expertise has been used to devise and deploy the solutions and services that keep our clients’ eCommerce sites running like clockwork, 24/7.

The core of everything we do is based on Respect for People. We don’t show respect by empowering people and letting them get on with their job. We show respect by challenging each one of us every step of the way.

Supreme brands such as END. Clothing, Fred Perry, Byredo, Diptyque Paris, Brewdog, Big Bus Tours, Selco, In The Style and O’Neills choose Akoova’s K-Hosting to run their Magento online stores on the cloud.
