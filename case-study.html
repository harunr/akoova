What was the biggest breakthrough you got from using Akoova?

The biggest breakthrough we got from using Akoova was to unburden our technical team from managing the whole operation of running Magento on AWS.

About 3 years ago we recruited an in-house dev-ops team and built a complex and highly scalable AWS cloud infrastructure.

This is a mission critical part of our fast growing business.

Had we gone with Akoova in the first place it would have allowed us to focus on innovation.
