The K-Hosting Model

Knowledge feeds our continuous quest to improve the K-Hosting Platform. If a problem occurs, our DevOps Team steps in to investigate and fix the problem. Their newly-acquired knowledge is then fed into a Knowledge Base, which in turn is then invested back into the K-Hosting Platform.

  • Code enhancements, such as monitoring tools to ensure that future problems are detected and resolved before the client is even aware.
  • Transparent collaboration. Knowledge is shared both within our internal teams and externally to our client.

This problem-solving cycle allows us to constantly evolve and enhance the K-Hosting Platform for all our clients so that those problems don’t occur anymore in the first place.

Made robust with AWS
