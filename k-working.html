Respect & Value

Discover how our people are at the heart of our platform, and are empowered to make the best decisions for you. Automated solutions aren’t built by automating processes; they evolve from understanding your day-to-day demands.

Before we build cloud platforms .. we build our people

At Akoova, we value ingenuity and give our people room to use it. Everyone we employ is respected as a potential problem-solver. We empower the people closest to the work because they are best to assess the issue, as well as provide the answers.

Automating solutions doesn’t come from automating our processes.

Understanding our clients, their ambitions and their business is all part of delivering technology that truly adds value. Our DevOps concierge service is a case in point. There are no passed bucks or pointing fingers. It’s an open-ended, open-minded affair.

Knowledge isn’t power when you don’t share it.

Our policy is to spread it far and wide: within our teams, with our clients and with their developers.

K-Working: Our platform is premium our people make it supreme

We approach problem-solving as a collaborative process with a shared responsibility. No matter how brilliant our platform is, it’s people that solve the problems.
